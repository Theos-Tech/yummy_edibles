Executive Summary

Business Name: Yummy Edibles

Location: Lagos, Nigeria

Business Type: Cake Bakery and Training Services

Founder: Ayomide Ibigbami

Mission: To delight customers with delicious, high-quality cakes while empowering aspiring bakers with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the baking industry.

Vision: To become the leading cake bakery and baking academy in Lagos, renowned for creativity, taste, and excellence.

Goals:

  • Establish a loyal customer base for the bakery within the first year.
  • Launch a comprehensive baking training program within six months.
  • Achieve a revenue target of ₦30 million by the end of the second year.

Business Description

Yummy Edibles focuses on two main areas:

  • Cake Bakery: Custom cakes for special occasions, pastries, cupcakes, and dessert catering services.
  • Baking Training & Course Sales: Offering basic and advanced cake decoration courses, online and in-person workshops, and specialized courses on cake business management.

Market Analysis

Target Market:

  • Individuals celebrating weddings, birthdays, and special occasions.
  • Corporate clients needing custom cakes for events.
  • Aspiring bakers looking to learn cake baking and decoration skills.
  • Event planners needing high-quality cakes for their clients.

Industry Analysis:

The cake bakery industry in Lagos is competitive but has high demand, especially for custom cakes. Baking classes and courses are growing in popularity as more people look to start their own baking businesses or improve their skills. The trend of home baking businesses is on the rise, creating an opportunity to tap into the training market.

Our Services

Cake Bakery

Custom cakes for weddings, birthdays, and corporate events, as well as a variety of pastries and cupcakes.

Baking Training

Offering both in-person and online courses on cake baking and decoration, plus specialized workshops for aspiring bakers.

Event Catering

Dessert catering services for all types of events, ensuring your guests enjoy a sweet experience.

Organization and Management

Founder: Ayomide Ibigbami

Key Personnel:

  • Head Baker: Manages the baking team and oversees production quality.
  • Marketing Manager: Develops and executes marketing strategies.
  • Training Coordinator: Oversees the course content and training delivery.
  • Finance Manager: Manages financial planning, budgeting, and reporting.

Business Structure: Limited Liability Company (LLC)

Marketing and Sales Strategy

Brand Identity:

  • Brand Name: Yummy Edibles
  • Logo & Colors: Cheerful, vibrant design with pink, gold, and white colors symbolizing sweetness and celebration.
  • Tagline: "Baking Your Dreams into Reality"

Marketing Channels:

  • Website: Central hub for showcasing products, taking orders, and course registration.
  • Social Media: Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and Twitter for sharing photos, baking tips, and engaging with customers.

Sales Strategy:

  • Direct Sales: Through the bakery location for walk-ins and event catering.
  • Online Sales: Orders placed via the website for delivery or pickup.
  • Training Course Sales: Promote through social media, partnerships with culinary schools, and targeted ads.
  • Partnerships: Collaborate with event planners, wedding venues, and corporate clients.

Financial Plan
